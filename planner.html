A short, optional tool to help a facilitator decide when shorter check-ins may be useful across the school year.

How this works

Answer as few or as many questions as you like. The planner suggests a starting check-in based on your current PLC context, and, if you add a start date, sketches approximate windows across the year.

This is guidance, not a required schedule. Check-ins can be used independently and do not need to be completed in order. Choose one next step rather than trying to address every domain at once.

Privacy note: Nothing on this page is stored or transmitted. Your inputs stay in your browser and disappear when you close the tab.
