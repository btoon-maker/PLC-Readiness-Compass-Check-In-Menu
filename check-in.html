Pick the check-in that fits where your PLC is right now

The full Compass is still available as a comprehensive beginning-of-year or midyear readiness assessment. The shorter check-ins each focus on one readiness condition, so a team can reflect without fatigue.

Check-ins can be used independently and do not need to be completed in order. Not sure where to start? The PLC Year Planner can suggest one.

Privacy note: This page does not store or transmit your responses. Your results stay in your browser unless you choose to copy and share your response code with your PLC facilitator.

Check-In

This is not meant to grade your PLC or evaluate individual teachers. It is meant to help each person pause and notice which conditions are helping the PLC function, and which may need attention before deeper collaborative inquiry.

Privacy note: Your responses stay in your browser. Only the response code is shareable, and your written evidence notes are never included in it.

Rating Scale

1 → Not Yet EvidentRarely or not yet visible in our PLC work.
2 → EmergingHappens sometimes, but not consistently yet.
3 → DevelopingPresent and helpful, but still needs strengthening.
4 → EstablishedA regular part of how our PLC works.
