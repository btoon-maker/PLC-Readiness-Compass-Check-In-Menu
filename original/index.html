Before You Begin

This is not meant to grade your PLC or evaluate individual teachers.

It is meant to help each person pause and think: What conditions are helping our PLC function as a professional learning community, and what conditions may need attention before we can engage in deeper collaborative inquiry?

Answer based on what you have actually experienced in your most recent PLC meetings. When possible, think of a concrete example before choosing a rating.

Rating Scale

3 → DevelopingPresent and helpful, but still needs strengthening.
