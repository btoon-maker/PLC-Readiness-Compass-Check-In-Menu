What the PLC Readiness Compass is

The PLC Readiness Compass is a flexible reflection and facilitation hub that helps professional learning communities notice readiness conditions, guide honest conversation, and choose one meaningful next step.

What it is not: not teacher evaluation, not a compliance checklist, not a psychometric test. Scores are conversation starters, not measurement. Nothing is stored or transmitted unless someone chooses to share a response code.

Five Readiness Areas

The conditions the Compass is built around. A short check-in looks at one; the full Compass looks at all five.

Shared Leadership

Teacher voice and shared ownership, even when a lead or administrator guides the agenda.

Shared Purpose

A shared reason for meeting that connects to student learning.

Collective Learning

Using evidence, trying ideas, and following up on what helped students.

Shared Practice

Making real teaching practice visible to one another in low-risk ways.

Supportive Conditions

The time, trust, and norms that let PLC work actually happen.

“Shared Purpose” is our label for Hord and Sommers’ “Shared Values and Vision.” Full framework language is in the Facilitator Guide.

How the Process Works

Reflection comes first; the dashboard is step three, not the starting point.

1

Reflect privately

Each member rates privately, thinking of evidence first.

2

Share voluntarily

Members choose whether to share a response code; written notes stay private.

3

Generate a team report

The dashboard turns shared codes into a report of patterns and perception gaps.

4

Choose one next step

The team picks one realistic move for the next cycle — not a fix for everything.

Read the Guide
Not sure whether to use the Full Compass or a Short Check-In?

Use the Full Compass when…

  • You are starting or restarting PLC work.
  • It is the beginning of the year or midyear.
  • The team needs a broad readiness baseline.
  • You want to look across all five readiness areas.

Use a Short Check-In when…

  • The team only has 10–15 minutes.
  • One readiness condition needs attention.
  • You want to reduce fatigue.
  • The team is in the middle of an ongoing PLC cycle.

Both live in the Check-In Menu — the full Compass is built in, so there is no separate version to open.
